Method: daily on-chain scan of native-KUB transfers between the Bitkub Exchange wallets and external addresses. Inflow = deposits onto the exchange (often precede selling); Outflow = withdrawals to self-custody (accumulation). Transfers with other Bitkub wallets — hot/cold rebalancing, kubscan-tagged Bitkub addresses, and known operational/treasury wallets — are excluded, so only genuine external flow is counted. Baht uses the current KUB price. Direct native transfers only — for analysis, not investment advice.
